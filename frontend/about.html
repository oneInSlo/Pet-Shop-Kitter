Welcome To Kitter!

At Kitter, we believe that pets aren't just animals – they're cherished members of the family. With a deep-rooted passion for animal welfare and a commitment to providing exceptional care, we're dedicated to being your partner in every step of your pet parenting journey. From puppies to parrots, kittens to fish, we're here to provide a haven where every creature can thrive.

Our Story

Kitter was born out of a shared love for animals and a desire to make a positive impact on the lives of pets and their owners. Our founders, a team of dedicated animal enthusiasts with backgrounds in veterinary medicine, pet nutrition, and animal behavior, came together with a vision to create a haven where pets and their humans could thrive together. What started as a small pet store has evolved into a beloved community hub, where pet lovers come together to share their passion and expertise.

Our Philosophy

At Kitter, we believe in holistic pet care that nurtures the physical, emotional, and social well-being of every animal. We understand that each pet is unique, with their own personality, preferences, and needs. That's why we take a personalized approach to pet care, offering a curated selection of high-quality products and services tailored to meet the specific needs of your furry, feathered, or scaly friend. From premium pet foods and accessories to expert grooming and healthcare advice, we're here to provide everything you need to give your pet the happy, healthy life they deserve.

Our Promise

When you choose Kitter, you're not just choosing a store – you're choosing a partner who shares your passion for pets and is committed to their well-being. Whether you're a first-time pet parent or a seasoned enthusiast, we're here to provide guidance, support, and inspiration every step of the way. Our knowledgeable staff members are always on hand to answer your questions, offer advice, and help you find the perfect products and services for your pet.

Community Engagement

At the heart of Kitter is our commitment to building a strong and supportive pet-loving community. We believe that pets have the power to bring people together, and we're dedicated to fostering connections between pets and their owners. From educational workshops and adoption events to community outreach programs and charitable initiatives, we're proud to be an active and engaged member of the communities we serve.

Get In Touch

We invite you to explore our store and experience the Kitter difference for yourself. Whether you're in need of pet supplies, grooming services, or simply a friendly chat with fellow pet lovers, we're here to welcome you with open arms. Visit us in-store, give us a call, or connect with us online – we can't wait to meet you and your beloved pets!
